Eligibility Criteria

The Candidates having passed the Matriculation examination are eligible to seek admission in the polytechnic. Counselling and seat allotment of Punjab Polytechnic Admission will be based on aggregate marks of Maths, Science and English in Class 10 exam;

Counselling - Document Checklist

  1. Marks Sheet of Qualifying Examination
  2. Character Certificate
  3. Matriculation Certificates as proof of Date of Birth
  4. Proof of Residence/Domicile Certificate
  5. Category Certificate
  6. Income Certificate, if admitted under Tuition Fee waiver seat / Reserved Category
  7. Two recent passport size photographs

Leet Entry

10 percent of total seats are reserved for Lateral entry students in second year of Diploma.

Vacant seats of first year Diplima Course are filled through LEET.
