VISION

The vision of the Electrical Engineering Department is to provide world clean technical education with the objective of molding the youth into enlightened citizens, who will work for the betterment of society.

QUALITY POLICY

To impart and ensure value based Quality Education and mould the inherent talent of youth into skills required in the dynamic and highly competitive world of technology.

GOALS

  • Produce disciplined creative and quality engineer
  • To provide students with hands-on engineering experience through laboratory courses & project.
  • To cultivate entrepreneurship spirit in students.
  • To provide every student with a strong conceptual foundation in the core areas of our field.
  • To coach students to work in teams and provide insight and direction into the atmosphere as might be encountered by professionals in the field.
